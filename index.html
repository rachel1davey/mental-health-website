What is Mental Health?

Mental health affects how we think, feel, and act. Understanding it helps us care for ourselves and others.

Common Issues

Anxiety

Feeling nervous, tense or restless.

Depression

Persistent sadness or loss of interest.

Burnout

Exhaustion from chronic stress or overwork.

Stress

Pressure from life’s challenges.

Managing Stress & Emotions

  • 🧘‍♀️ Practice mindfulness or meditation
  • 📖 Journal your thoughts
  • 🚶‍♂️ Go for short walks
  • 📞 Talk to a trusted friend
  • 🎨 Try creative activities
