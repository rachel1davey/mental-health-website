🧠 What Is Mental Health?

Mental health shapes how we think, feel, and act. It's the foundation of how we handle stress, relate to others, and make decisions. By understanding mental health, we can better support ourselves and those around us.

Common Issues

Here are a few challenges people often face. Awareness is the first step toward healing:

🌀 Anxiety

Feelings of worry or fear that are strong enough to interfere with daily life.

🌧️ Depression

Persistent sadness, loss of interest, and a sense of hopelessness that can impact every area of life.

🌪️ Stress

Feelings of being overwhelmed or unable to cope with mental or emotional pressure.

🔥 Burnout

Physical, emotional, and mental exhaustion caused by prolonged stress or overwork.

🌙 Loneliness

Feeling isolated or disconnected from others, even when surrounded by people.

💡 Low Self-Esteem

Struggling with self-worth, confidence, or feeling inadequate.

Please be aware that these are not the only issues people suffering with mental health may face. It's important to seek help and support. Never feel ashamed even if you feel like your issues are more 'complex'.

Tips for your Mental Health ❤️

A good night’s sleep helps regulate emotions, improves focus, and boosts overall mood. Aim for 7–9 hours per night.

Try deep breathing, journaling, or short meditations to reduce anxiety and stay present in the moment.

Talk to friends, family, or a therapist. You don’t have to go through difficult times alone.
